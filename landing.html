Vincent Bouyssounouse Interactive Media - Art - Design


Evil Born of Love is a visual novel that uses Twine text-based framework in conjunction with looping 3D animations created in Blender. The novel deals with themes of growth, identity, spirtuality, and overcoming hardship. The 3D visuals are used as a backdrop while players process each segment of the story, deciding which paths to take.

For the project I created over 50 seamless looping animations with modeled, rigged, and animated human and animal characters placed in custom environments with architectural and natural elements. In order to fit the emotional themes and warped narration style, I animated shader properites, scene and world lighting, and camera properties. Non-looping animations were utilized to move players to new environments and for key plot moments.

The game was created as a project for the senior sequence
in the Art and Design, Games/Playable Media department at U.C. Santa Cruz.
