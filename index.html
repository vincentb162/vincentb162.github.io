About Me

Art & Design: Games & Playable BA from University of California, Santa Cruz

I grew up in Palo Alto, California and have enjoyed artwork and design my whole life. I am passionate about the power of interactive digital media to entertain, educate, and change the world.

Through my work in the Games & Playable Media department at U.C. Santa Cruz, I have gained skills in various aspects of game development/design - from script development and object oriented programming in Javascript and Python to 3D modeling, texturing, and rigging using Maya, Blender, and Adobe Substance Painter. I find working with others exciting and rewarding- I believe my openness towards others ideas as well as my dedication towards properly managed communication and expectations makes me a great asset to teams.

My hobbies include yoga, playing piano, mountain biking, and jigsaw puzzles.
