About Me

Art & Design: Games & Playable BA from University of California, Santa Cruz

Growing up in Palo Alto, programming and interactive digital media have always been a big part of my life. I remember the rush I felt when creating my first boxy 3D model - I plotted the points by hand in a text file, and the feeling of my brain starting to visualize the 3D object felt like pure magic. My love for computer graphics led me to the Games & Playable Media department at U.C. Santa Cruz where I developed my skills in player, enemy AI, and environmental script development and solidified my OOP skills. I also gained proficiency in 3D modeling, texturing, and rigging using Maya, Blender, and Adobe Substance Painter. Since I recieved my BA from Santa Cruz, I have dived further into Unity and Godot game engines, and will complete my third quarter of C++ at Foothill Community College in June 2025. I am a strong team player, and excel in collaborative environments. My years of experience crafting commisioned projects for dozens of clients have honed my communication skills, and ability to adapt to any project.

My hobbies include piano, mountain biking, and jigsaw puzzles.
