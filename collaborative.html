The Lost Robot is a sci-fi dungeon crawler made with Phaser 3 game engine in collaboration Dongling Yang, Michael Leung, and Vincent Kurniadjaja. The player controls a robot that explores a high tech facility, working to avoid enemies, solve puzzles, and collect upgrades. The game is avaliable to play on Itch.io

For the game I created a custom particle systems for VFX, created animation management based on player movement/interaction with teleport blocks.

Spiritual Relief is an endless runner made in Phaser 3 featuring click to location movement and waves of enemies that vary in movement and speed. The player loses health from bumping into the enemies, and health can be regained by playing the embedded mini-game on the bottom half of the screen, which involves clicking hearts that randomly appear in a grid. For Spiritual Relief I developed scripts for enemy spawning/movement as well as player movement. Made in collaboration with Dongling Yang, Michael Leung, and Vincent Kurniadjaja. Play on Itch.io
