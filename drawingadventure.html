Painting Adventure is a 2D sidescroller game made using Phaser 3 Game engine. The game progresses through 2 guided art lessons where players interact with an instructor who works to boost their confidence in their artistic ability. Players are able to paint using a mouse or drawing tablet on various surfaces within the game. The game is avaliable to play on Itch.io

After completing their lessons, the player then is free to roam outside, where they find they can paint on various objects around them, including trees and wandering animals. The game is designed to help players reflect on their own concept of art, and how they define artistic/creative ability.
